Featured tips

The Big 3

these tools are essential for getting through 80% of calls that are not a huge problem most other tools should be accessible from your shop at your job

  1. 14 in 1 screwdriver
  2. 6 or 8 inch adjustable wrench
  3. needle nose pliers with wire cutters

these alone will solve a majority of your problems
clogged toilets? your job should have plenty of plungers
busted outlet chances are they have that already too though i recommend getting your own soon after you start

Listen BE COACHABLE

pride and ego are some things that wont get you very far in this line of work
be ready to learn things over and over a lot of experienced techs have their own way of doing things use the best for you

things new maintenance techs get wrong

most new guys or gals tend to overlook these things.

  1. Not double checking their work
  2. not paying attention to details
  3. not tracking their work whether a pamphlet or journal (always track your work)
